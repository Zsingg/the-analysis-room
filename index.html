Yes. Mr Tan has taken homeschooled students and retakers through the full syllabus, including students with no school support.

How do online lessons run?

Lessons are 2 hours each and run over Google Meet or Zoom, with shared notes and annotated scripts.

Do essay marking credits expire?

Credits from the 10-Credit Bundle are valid for 4 months from the date of purchase. Pay-Per-Mark and Unlimited Monthly have no credits to expire.

Can I get my essay or SBQ marked faster than 48 hours?

Yes. A 24-hour rush turnaround is available on any marking tier for a flat +$15 per submission.
